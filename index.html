About Me

I live in Chicago with my lovely wife and two kids, and work as a Product Manager for Amazon Web Services, supporting developers using AWS. This is a tremendously fun and rewarding job, giving me the challenging task of working closely with some of the smartest people in the world while serving the needs of millions of customers.

I am an avid reader, a keen observer of technology, and am generally interested in long-term trends in society and current events. I also enjoy lifting heavy weights and running slowly for long distances.
